Rahul Sen

Rahul started Crome Hair Studio after getting fed of all the same old hair studios in Bhopal and wanted to give something new to the people of Bhopal. He started cutting hair when he was 13 years old and been cutting hair for almost 13 years now.

He introduced many new techniques and hairstyles in Bhopal including Dreadlock. Only two people have LOREAL's certificate in the whole city and Rahul is one of them. He is officially sponsered by LOREAL. He has currently two hair studios but is expanding everyday.

He came from nothing and made a name for himsef and his beloved hair studio. Rahul loves Crome hair studio more than anything and devoted his whole life to it.
